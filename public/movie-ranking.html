The Best Movies According To Mustafa

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lord of The Rings

A fellowship of diverse heroes embarks on an epic journey to destroy an evil ring and save Middle-earth from darkness.

Harry Potter

A young wizard discovers his magical heritage and embarks on a journey to defeat a dark wizard who threatens the wizarding world.

The Schindler's List

It's a powerful film about a businessman who saves over a thousand Jews during the Holocaust by employing them in his factories.
